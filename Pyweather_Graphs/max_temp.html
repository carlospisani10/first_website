Max Temperature Analysis

Temperature decreases as Latitude increases for this instance. THis makes sense as the analysis was done on November 15, 2017, so the Southern Hemisphere is approaching their summer solstice while the Northen Hemisphere approaches their winter solstice. It is likely that if another analysis was done on May 15th, 2018, the graph would be flipped. Temperatures around the equator are high and will remain relatively constant year around.
