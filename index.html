Project: Latitude Effects on Weather

In this project, we analyzed how Latitude affects different aspects of weather. Focusing on temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity on 500 random cities; we used a variety of tools to query, format, and visualize the data.


Utilizing the openweathermapy API, data was pulled on the cities, which was then organized in a Pandas dataframe. Lastly, Matplotlib was utilized to populate the graphs after the data was organized. This website illustrates our efforts and provides explenations to the analysis.
